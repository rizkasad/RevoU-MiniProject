Profile Company


Blue Vinyl (블루바이닐) is a record label founded by South Korean singer Baek Yerin (백예린). The label was founded on November 6th, 2019, and currently houses Baek Yerin, Cloud, and The Volunteers.

Vision

To create a good music.

Mission

- Making a good music.
- Serving a good performance for all of the fans around the world.
